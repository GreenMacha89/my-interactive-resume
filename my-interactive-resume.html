7. Strategic insights & action points
Gap — urgent

GEO / AEO absent from CV. Present in 8/10 SEO Manager offers. If no hands-on experience yet, this is the most high-return upskilling area before applying to SEO-track roles.

Gap — urgent

Schema markup / structured data not mentioned anywhere in the CV. Cited in 6/10 SEO offers. Add if genuine experience exists at La Salle or previous roles.

Differentiator — underused

BigQuery appears in 0/10 SEO Manager offers as a requirement — meaning having it is a genuine edge over most candidates. Frame it as "data depth rare in a marketing profile."

Differentiator — underused

Audiovisual / editorial background is a direct asset for SEO Manager roles at IRA Financial, Inkitt, and LGC, which explicitly require content creation and optimisation ability — not just auditing.

Confirmed strength

+42% organic lead growth over 5 years at La Salle works for both profiles: proves organic impact for SEO Manager, and funnel ownership with measurable result for Growth / Digital Marketing Manager.

Confirmed strength

Dual EdTech background (La Salle + Zigurat) is rare and directly valued by Codeway and MyWellbeing, both of which cite "marketing a product that requires education before purchase" as a key requirement.
